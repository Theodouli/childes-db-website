Frequency

The frequency tool shows word counts over all utterances marked as belonging to a particular speaker.

Derived Measures

This analysis shows growth curves for various lexical measures including Mean Length of Utterance in words (MLU-w) and Type-Token Ratio (TTR). Measures are computed over all utterances marked as belonging to a particular speaker.

Population Properties

This analysis plots the number of utterances or word tokens by particular speakers in particular corpora.
